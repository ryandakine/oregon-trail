Fort Kearney
A military outpost on the south bank of the Platte River, established just last year to protect emigrants on the Oregon Trail. The parade ground is busy with soldiers and travelers. A blacksmith works steadily at his forge, and a sutler's store offers supplies at frontier prices.
"The post is but one year old, and already it bears the look of a place that has seen too many pass through and too few remain."
— Lt. Daniel Woodbury, 1848
